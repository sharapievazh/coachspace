Privacy Policy — CoachSpace

Last updated: July 6, 2026

CoachSpace is a professional coaching workspace application designed for coaches and their clients.

Data Collection

CoachSpace does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered in the app (session notes, client names, goals, assessments) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

CoachSpace does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Audio and Screen

The app uses your device's audio capabilities solely to play session timer alerts. The Wake Lock feature keeps your screen active during sessions. Neither audio nor screen data is recorded or transmitted.

Data Storage

All app data is stored in your device's local storage and is never uploaded to external servers.
